IIE Harvard Anglia Formatting Tips

  • Book and journal titles should appear in italics.
  • Always include an access date for online sources.
  • Do not include URLs in in-text citations.
  • Use sentence case for article and webpage titles.
  • Check punctuation carefully.
  • Use "Available at:" before URLs.
  • Ensure author names are presented correctly.
  • Use the Reference Guide page for formatting examples.
  • Use the In-Text Citation Guide page for citation examples.

Why Use the Generator?

This generator was designed specifically to support students using the IIE Harvard Anglia referencing style.

The generator helps reduce formatting mistakes by automatically creating reference list entries from information supplied by the user.

Students can also copy generated references directly into assignments, improving efficiency and supporting collaborative academic work.
